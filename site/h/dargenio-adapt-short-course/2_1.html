2两天课程的主线

D'Argenio ADAPT Short Course: Dynamic Systems, Estimation, PBPK, and Population Simulation


2.1 课程目标 [1: 00:05:50--00:15:28]
Day 1 的开场很重要。D’Argenio 明确说，虽然这是 ADAPT short course，
但真正要学的不是某个菜单在哪里，而是 general modeling concepts：dynamic
systems、simulation、estimation、statistical error model、debugging、population
uncertainty。这些概念会超越任何一个具体软件。
Figure 2: [1: 00:11:16] Day 1 agenda: modeling background, simulation,
estimation, debugging.
Day 1 的路线是：
(i) ADAPT 的 systems modeling framework.
(ii) Direct/indirect response simulation.
(iii) TMDD simulation.
(iv) Least squares, maximum likelihood, Bayesian estimation.
(v) Indirect response model 的 individual estimation.
(vi) Debugging and advanced coding.
Day 2 的路线是：
(i) Simple PBPK/circulatory model simulation.
(ii) Minimal PBPK with naive pooled data estimation.
(iii) ACI/background batch mode.
(iv) Population simulation.
(v) 午休后才进入 hierarchical/population modeling；目前这两份 transcript
没有包含下午部分。
