9.3 The rate-limiting-step question
Whenever concentration and response are disconnected, ask:
What is the slowest step
between plasma concentration and observed response?
Candidate answers include:
– absorption or formulation release,
– distribution to the site of action,
– receptor binding or target turnover,
– endogenous mediator turnover,
– signal transduction cascade,
– cell turnover or disease progression,
– tolerance, adaptation, or feedback.
