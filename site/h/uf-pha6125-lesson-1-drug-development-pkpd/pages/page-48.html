11.3 ICH M15: question of interest
ICH M15’s most useful phrase for students is “question of interest” [8]. A model
should not be defended in the abstract. It should be defended relative to the
question it answers:
– Can the next dose level be escalated safely?
– Which Phase 2b doses should be studied?
– Can renal impairment be handled by dose adjustment rather than a dedi-
cated outcome trial?
– Is exposure in pediatric patients matched to adult ecacious exposure?
– Does a proposed label statement follow from observed exposure–response?
The required model credibility depends on decision impact and consequence of
wrong decision.
