This is bookkeeping with physiological meaning. If renal clearance falls because
kidney function declines, total clearance falls unless another pathway compen-
sates. If metabolism is saturated, CL may no longer be constant. Phenytoin is
the standard classroom example: therapeutic concentrations can approach the
nonlinear Michaelis–Menten region.
