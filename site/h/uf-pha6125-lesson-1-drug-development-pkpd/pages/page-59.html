14.3 A personal checklist
For every future PHA 6125 case, I will ask:
– What is the decision: continue, stop, dose, design, or label?
– What is observed, and what is latent?
– Is the key relationship dose–response, exposure–response, or response over
time?
– Which parameter controls exposure: CL, V
d
, F , k
a

– Is the PD direct, delayed, indirect, irreversible, or disease-mediated?
– What covariates could change PK or PD variability?
– What would make a negative result interpretable rather than ambiguous?
