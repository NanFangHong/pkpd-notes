5.2 A label statement is a compressed model
When a label says “reduce the dose in severe renal impairment,” it is hiding a
chain of reasoning:
renal function → CL → AUC → safety/ecacy → dose adjustment.
When a label says “no dose adjustment is required,” it hides the same chain but
reaches the opposite decision. This is why Phase 3 learning analyses matter:
they support not only when adjustment is needed, but also when it is not needed.
