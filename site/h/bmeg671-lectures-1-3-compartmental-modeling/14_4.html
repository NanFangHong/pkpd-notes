UD BMEG 671 Lectures 1-3: Compartmental Modeling Foundations


14.4 Before believing the model
Validate:
– compare predictions with data not used only for coding checks;
– inspect residuals or systematic deviations;
– ask whether the model answers the intended question;
– state which mechanisms are included and which are deliberately lumped;
– communicate uncertainty and limitations.
