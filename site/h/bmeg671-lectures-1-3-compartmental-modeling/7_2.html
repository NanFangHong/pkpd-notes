7.2 ODE function shape
The canonical shape is:
function derivVector = ODEs(t,y)
q1 = y(1);
q2 = y(2);
dq1dt = ...;
dq2dt = ...;
derivVector = [dq1dt,dq2dt]';
end
The solver does not care what the states mean. It receives a vector and returns
a derivative vector. The meaning is carried by our naming, equations, and
documentation.
