59.4 Step 4: identify the approximation
Ask how the hard integral or posterior is approximated. If the answer is FOCE,
think local approximation. If SAEM, think stochastic EM. If MCMC, think pos-
terior simulation. If bootstrap, think repeated resampling. The approximation
determines what errors or diagnostics matter.
