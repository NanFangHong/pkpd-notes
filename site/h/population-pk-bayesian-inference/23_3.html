23.3 SAEM versus exact EM
Exact EM can be imagined as:
Replace missing parameters by their exact conditional expectations.
SAEM is:
Simulate plausible missing parameters and average them carefully
over iterations.
Monte Carlo gives access to complex nonlinear posteriors; stochastic approxi-
mation controls noise across iterations.
