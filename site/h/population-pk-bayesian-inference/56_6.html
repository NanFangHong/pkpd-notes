Population PK as Bayesian Inference


56.6 The EM algorithm
The EM unit is one of the most important bridges to Lavielle. It shows how to
maximize an observed likelihood when some variables are hidden. In PopPK,
the hidden variables are individual parameters. The observed likelihood requires
integrating them out; the complete-data likelihood would be easier if they were
known.
The EM slogan for this note is:
alternate between imagining latent individuals and updating population parameters.
SAEM modies the imagining step by simulation and stochastic approximation.
