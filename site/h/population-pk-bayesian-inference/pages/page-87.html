23.3 FO, FOCE, Laplace as approximations
For intuition:
– FO linearizes the nonlinear model around η
i
= 0. It is fast but can be
crude when nonlinearity or variability is large.
– FOCE linearizes around each individual’s conditional estimate bη
i
. This
respects individual information better.
– Laplace approximates the individual integral near the conditional mode,
using local curvature.
– SAEM uses simulation and stochastic approximation rather than only local
linearization.
Remark. My translation: NONMEM classical methods are local approxima-
tions to the same Bayesian-looking integral; SAEM is a stochastic route through
the same landscape.
