41.2 Visual predictive checks
A VPC is not merely a plot. It is a comparison between observed summaries
and predictive summaries under the model. The reader should ask:
(i) Are simulations using the same design as the observed study?
(ii) Are covariate groups respected?
(iii) Are BLQ and missing data mechanisms handled consistently?
(iv) Are prediction intervals checking the part of the distribution that matters
clinically?
(v) Does the VPC hide individual-level failure by pooling too aggressively?

predictive distribution.
