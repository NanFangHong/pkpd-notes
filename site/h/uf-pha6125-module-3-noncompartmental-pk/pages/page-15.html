3.3 Units
AUC has concentration times time units. If concentration is mg/L and time is
hours, then
AUC has units mg · h/L.
Clearance follows by dimensional cancellation:
D
AUC
=
mg
mg · h/L
= L/h.
This is a useful self-check. If your CL output has impossible units, the bug
is probably in dose units, concentration units, time units, or bioavailability
interpretation.
