2.1 One sentence
The whole path can be compressed into one sentence:
A complex mechanistic model is a map from parameters to pre-
dictions; linear algebra studies the local derivative of that map,
ODE/LTI theory gives its dynamical form, numerical analysis tells
us when the derivative is trustworthy, information geometry turns
the derivative into a metric, and MBAM follows that metric to sim-
plify the model without losing the predictions we care about.
This sentence is dense, so the note expands it slowly.
