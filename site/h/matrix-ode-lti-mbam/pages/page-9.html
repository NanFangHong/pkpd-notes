2.4 Why MBAM sits at the end
MBAM feels advanced because it presupposes many languages at once:
model
↓
predictions
↓
sensitivity matrix
↓
metric
↓
geodesic
↓
limiting approximation
Each arrow is a course by itself. Matrix tricks explain the sensitivity matrix.
ODE and LTI explain what a mechanistic model is. Numerical linear algebra
explains why small singular values should make us cautious. Information theory
explains why Fisher information is not arbitrary. MBAM then turns all of this
into a model-reduction workow.
