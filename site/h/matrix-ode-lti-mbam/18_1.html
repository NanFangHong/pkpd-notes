18Connection back to pharmacology

From Matrix Tricks to MBAM


18.1 Why this belongs next to PK/PD
Population PK taught us that individual parameters are latent and inferred
through a hierarchical model. MBAM teaches a complementary lesson: mecha-
nistic parameters may be too detailed for the prediction task, and the meaningful
objects may be combinations living on a lower-dimensional manifold.
In PK/PD terms:
rich mechanism
↓
prediction map
↓
active combinations
↓
decision-ready reduced model
This is exactly what we want when moving from detailed QSP to dosing, target
discovery, biomarker selection, or trial design.
