16.3 Before believing the result
Ask:
Is the observed behavior a property of the model mechanism, a
property of the chosen parameter values, a property of the initial
condition, or a property of the numerical implementation?
This one sentence catches many errors. A stable equilibrium under one param-
eter set may disappear under another. A closed orbit may be a neutral Lotka–
Volterra orbit rather than an attracting rhythm. A negative state may come
from a structurally invalid model or from a solver step crossing a boundary. A
beautiful SimBiology diagram may still encode the wrong rate law.
