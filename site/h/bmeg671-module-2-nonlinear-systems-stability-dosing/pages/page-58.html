14.2 Why dose objects are useful in SimBiology
SimBiology provides dose objects: target species, start time, interval, amount or
rate, and repeat count. The Sep 24 transcript highlights a practical advantage:
repeated dosing can be represented without manually splicing time intervals.
For simple single scripts, manual segmentation is transparent. For repeated
regimens, unit-checked dose objects are safer.
