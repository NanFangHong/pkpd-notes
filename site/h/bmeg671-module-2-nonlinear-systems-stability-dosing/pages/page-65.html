15.3 What failure of nonnegative invariance means
If a state represents amount, concentration, population, or probability, negative
values are not physically meaningful. If the nonnegative orthant is not positively
invariant, then one of three things is usually true:
– the model was intended only for a smaller domain;
– a missing nonlinear term or boundary mechanism should be added;
– the simulation or parameter values are being used outside their credible
range.
This is not merely a mathematical aw. It is a biological warning.
