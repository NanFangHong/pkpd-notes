1.3 Why nonlinear behavior enters so early
Linear models are the cleanest possible classroom entrance. But real biomedical
systems become nonlinear almost immediately:
– infection terms often use products such as βSI;
– enzymatic elimination saturates like V
max
q/(K
m
+ q);
– receptor occupancy and signaling often have sigmoidal Hill functions;
– immune expansion, tumor growth, and cell populations are bounded by
resource limits;
– dose schedules are discontinuous in time;
– biological state variables must remain nonnegative.
The course therefore does not treat nonlinearity as advanced ornament. It is
the normal state of biomedical modeling.
