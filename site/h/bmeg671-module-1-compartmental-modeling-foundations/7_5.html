7.5 ode45 and ode15s
The transcript contrasts nonsti and sti solvers. In ordinary MATLAB usage,
ode45 is a default nonsti Runge–Kutta solver, while ode15s is commonly used
for sti systems [7, 8]. Stiness often appears when a model has both very fast
and very slow processes. Biomedical models frequently do: receptor binding
may equilibrate rapidly, while disease progression evolves over weeks.
The beginner lesson is not to memorize solver taxonomy. It is to watch for
solver warnings, step-size pathologies, impossible negative concentrations, and
sensitivity to tolerances.
