13.2 Good project shapes
Good early projects often have:
– a clear compartment diagram;
– a small state vector;
– documented parameter values or estimable parameters;
– data over time, not merely endpoint data;
– one or two scientically meaningful outputs;
– a reason to care about prediction or mechanism.
Examples include a simple PK/PD model, a tumor growth inhibition model,
a viral dynamics model, an immune response model, a glucose-insulin model, a
bone remodeling model, or a reduced QSP module.
