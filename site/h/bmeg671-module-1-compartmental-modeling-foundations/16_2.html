UD BMEG 671 Module 1: Compartmental Modeling Foundations


16.2 The sentence to keep
For any new biomedical model, ask:
What are the states, what moves between them, what is measured,
and what decision will this model support?
If that sentence is clear, the mathematics has a place to stand.
