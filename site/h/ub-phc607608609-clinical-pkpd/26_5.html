26.5 Lifespan, hematopoiesis, and odd-type outcomes
Cell lifespan models are natural when the observed response is a population of
cells that must age through compartments before appearing or disappearing.
Hematopoietic toxicity, neutrophil suppression, tumor growth inhibition, and
delayed recovery often need this language. The response is not delayed because
the assay is slow; it is delayed because biology has a production pipeline.
Not every PD endpoint is continuous. Categorical response, ordered tox-
icity grade, count data, and time-to-event outcomes need observation models
matched to the data type:
