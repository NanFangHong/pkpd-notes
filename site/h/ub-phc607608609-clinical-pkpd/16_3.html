16.3 Obesity
Obesity forces the distinction between loading and maintenance. Loading dose
depends on distribution; maintenance dose depends on clearance. Total body
weight, ideal body weight, adjusted body weight, lean body weight, and body
surface area are not interchangeable. The right body-size descriptor depends on
whether the drug distributes into adipose, lean tissue, extracellular water, or a
mixture.
