31.2 What to avoid
Avoid treating headings as isolated facts. Absorption, distribution, elimination,
and response are not chapters in nature. They are interfaces in the same dynami-
cal system. A transporter can aect absorption, hepatic uptake, renal secretion,
and tissue distribution. Protein binding can aect distribution, clearance, and
measurement. A biomarker delay can masquerade as slow distribution. Clin-
