Remark. Classical PK looks deterministic because many textbook equations
are deterministic. Clinical PK is not deterministic. The deterministic equation
is the forward map inside a larger uncertain system: uncertain patient, uncer-
tain adherence, uncertain sampling time, uncertain assay, uncertain response,
uncertain future.
