47.1 What the incomplete PHC 608 packet adds
The PHC 608 syllabus and representative lecture slides make the advanced-PK
bridge more explicit. The bridge is:
molecule and formulation → ADME mechanism
→ physiological or semi-mechanistic model
→ regimen consequence.
Structure-PK and QSPR should therefore be read before PBPK, not after it.
