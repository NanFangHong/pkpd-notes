33.3 Numerical sanity before inference
Before tting, simulate easy cases:
– zero dose should give zero drug concentration unless baseline drug exists;
– zero clearance should prevent elimination;
– larger dose should raise concentration under linear PK;
– larger clearance should lower AUC;
– larger volume should lower initial IV bolus concentration and lengthen half-
life if clearance is xed.
These checks are simple, but they catch sign errors, unit errors, wrong compart-
ment dosing, wrong output scaling, and bad initial conditions.
