19.3 NONMEM as an inference dialect
NONMEM’s THETA, ETA, and EPS syntax is not a separate theory. It is a
dialect for the hierarchy:
THETA ↔ population typical values and covariate eects,
ETA ↔ individual deviations,
EPS ↔ residual observation noise,
Ω, Σ ↔ variance components.
The objective function approximates a marginal likelihood where individual
random eects have been integrated out. FO, FOCE, Laplacian, SAEM, and
MCMC are dierent computational routes through the same conceptual prob-
lem.
