28.4 Deterministic and stochastic readings
ODEs are appropriate when concentrations or molecule numbers are high
enough that averaged rates are meaningful. Stochastic simulation becomes im-
portant when copy numbers are low, switching events matter, or cell-to-cell
variability is part of the biological question. The stochastic model is not a
decorative version of the ODE; it answers a dierent question:
mean trajectory versus distribution of possible trajectories.
