13.3 Maternal-fetal and other reversible exchange systems
Reversible systems are not limited to metabolism. Maternal-fetal transfer, dial-
ysis, tissue binding, intracellular trapping, and protein binding can all behave
like coupled reservoirs. The general question is:
does material leave the decision-relevant system,
or does it return on a clinically relevant time scale?
If it returns, a one-way clearance interpretation may be too blunt. If it does not
return, a loop model may overcomplicate the decision.
