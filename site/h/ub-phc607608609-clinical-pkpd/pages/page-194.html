Every QSP model should still be expressible as
dx

= f(x, u, θ, t), y = g(x, θ, t) + ϵ, a = δ(y, c).
The state equation is not enough. Observations and decisions complete the
model. A QSP model that cannot say what is observed and what decision it
informs is a mechanistic diagram, not yet a clinical pharmacology tool.
