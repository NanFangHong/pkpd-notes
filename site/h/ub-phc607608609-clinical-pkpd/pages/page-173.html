41.3 Oligonucleotides
Oligonucleotides have absorption, distribution, and PD features unlike classical
small molecules. They can have rapid plasma decline but long tissue half-life.
The relevant exposure may be tissue concentration or target RNA knockdown
rather than plasma AUC. Chemical modication, GalNAc targeting, renal clear-
ance, protein binding, and endosomal escape all become part of the PK/PD
chain.
